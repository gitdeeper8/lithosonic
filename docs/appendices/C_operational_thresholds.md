| Parameter | Symbol | Background | Elevated Alert | Critical |
|-----------|--------|------------|----------------|----------|
| Biot Coupling | B_c | < 0.55 | 0.55 - 0.75 | > 0.75 |
| Impedance Contrast | Z_c | < 0.20 | 0.20 - 0.45 | > 0.45 |
| Fracture Resonance | f_n | < 5% shift | 5-20% shift | > 20% or phase change |
| Attenuation | α_att | Q > 100 | 30 < Q < 100 | Q < 30 |

## LSI Alert Levels

| Level | LSI Range | Status | Action |
|-------|-----------|--------|--------|
| GREEN | < 0.60 | Background | Routine monitoring |
| YELLOW | 0.60 - 0.79 | Elevated Alert | Enhanced monitoring; stakeholder notification |
| RED | ≥ 0.80 | Active Instability | Emergency protocols; civil protection liaison |

## Site-Specific Calibration

The normalization bounds for each parameter are calibrated using the 14-year reference dataset from the Chicxulub reference site and adjusted for local conditions during the first 6 months of deployment at new sites.
