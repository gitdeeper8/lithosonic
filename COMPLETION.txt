KEY SCIENTIFIC FINDINGS
============================================================

1. Biot slow-wave diffusion tracks pore pressure fronts at
   ±50 m resolution without borehole instrumentation

2. Fracture resonance frequency inversion discriminates
   water/gas/magma with 89.3% accuracy

3. Acoustic emission rate Ṡ_ae precedes surface deformation
   by 18-52 days at volcanic sites

4. LSI achieves 92.7% accuracy with 24-day mean lead time
   (vs. 4-9 days for conventional monitoring)
